# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
"PME" (small and medium-sized enterprise) business owners who are curious about tech and business digitalization. They need clear guidance and assurance that Digicraft can effectively transform their digital presence and operations.

## Product Purpose
A landing website for Digicraft, a digital agency offering a comprehensive array of B2B services including websites, web apps, SaaS, desktop apps, mobile apps, flyers, logos, AI chatbots, AI agents, and automations. The site's goal is to convert PME curiosity into business inquiries by demonstrating high-end technological capability.

## Brand Personality
Young, fluid, and pro. The vibe should be dynamic, energetic, and smoothly professional—instilling confidence while feeling decidedly modern and fresh.

## Anti-references
Should NOT look like a generic, static, "dead," or "random AI-generated" template. It must feel alive, custom, intentional, and highly crafted.

## Design Principles
- **Show, Don't Just Tell**: Demonstrate the agency's capability (being "pro" and tech-savvy) through the interface's actual execution, precision, and motion, rather than just writing about it.
- **Dynamic Vitality**: The design must feel "living" and energetic (young and fluid) to actively counter the "dead AI" anti-pattern. Use purposeful motion and interactive elements.
- **Accessible Professionalism**: While young and tech-forward, the design must remain trustworthy, serious, and comprehensible to traditional PME owners looking to digitize.
- **Comprehensive yet Focused**: Present a wide variety of services (from flyers to complex AI agents) without overwhelming the user. The information architecture must be crystal clear.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
No specific accessibility requirements (standard web best practices apply).
